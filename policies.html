Our Policies

Financing

At EcoCrafts, we offer flexible financing options to make our eco-friendly products accessible to everyone. Customers can choose from:

  • Interest-free installments up to 6 months.
  • Discounted rates for bulk purchases.
  • Special financing offers during promotional events.

Delivery

We prioritize fast and eco-friendly delivery methods. Here’s what you can expect:

  • Free standard shipping for orders over $50.
  • Carbon-neutral delivery options available.
  • Same-day delivery in select areas for orders placed before 12 PM.
  • Orders typically arrive within 3-7 business days.

Return Policies

We strive to ensure customer satisfaction. If you’re not happy with your purchase, our return policies are here to help:

  • Returns accepted within 30 days of purchase with proof of receipt.
  • Items must be unused and in original packaging.
  • Refunds will be processed within 5-7 business days after the returned item is received.
  • Exchanges available for defective or damaged items at no additional cost.
